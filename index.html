What We Do

Two disciplines.
One integrated firm.

Auden Partners sits at the intersection of advice and capital. Our advisory professionals advise with an investor's mindset. Our investment professionals structure with an advisor's creativity. The result is a differentiated capability that neither a pure advisory firm nor a pure investment firm can replicate.

We work on a focused number of engagements, ensuring senior involvement at every stage — from the first conversation to closing. Our flexibility on structure and compensation reflects our commitment to aligning with the long-term interests of our clients and partners.

Restructuring & Liability Management

We advise companies and creditors on some of the most complex capital structure situations — in-court and out-of-court — with a track record spanning multiple market cycles.

  • Chapter 11 advisory
  • Out-of-court restructurings
  • Exchange offers & debt repurchases
  • Waivers, amendments & consent solicitations
  • Creditor & committee advisory

M&A and Capital Raising

We advise on the full range of strategic and financing transactions, bringing a principal investor's perspective to every engagement.

  • Mergers & acquisitions
  • Divestitures & corporate carveouts
  • Debt, equity & hybrid financings
  • Fairness opinions & valuation
  • Expert testimony & litigation support

Middle Market Private Equity

We partner with management teams to build value in North American middle market companies, with a flexible approach across the capital structure.

  • Platform investments (EBITDA $10M–$75M)
  • Family-owned business acquisitions
  • Corporate carveouts
  • Buy-and-build strategies
  • Defensible, cash-generative business models

Special Situations & Private Credit

We invest opportunistically in distressed and complex situations where our advisory expertise and structuring capabilities create a differentiated edge.

  • Distressed debt & equity
  • Asset-backed finance

Hybrid Capital Solutions

We invest our own capital alongside advisory engagements — a merchant banking model that enables creative solutions unavailable to traditional advisory-only or investment-only firms.

Aligned Advisory

When appropriate, we align our fee structure with our clients' outcomes — including deferred, contingent, or equity-based compensation — so our interests are structurally aligned with yours.

  • Flexible remuneration structures

  • Long-term equity participation
  • Management incentive design

Our Approach

Aligned interests.
Unconflicted advice.

Auden Partners was founded on a straightforward belief: the best outcomes come when advisors and investors share the same incentives as their clients. As a merchant bank, we can advise, invest, or do both — and we are transparent about which role we are playing in every situation.

We take on a focused number of mandates. Our senior professionals are involved in every aspect of every engagement — not just the pitch. We work discreetly, and we bring creative, unconventional thinking to situations where conventional approaches have fallen short.

Our professionals have led some of the most complex and consequential transactions in recent history — restructurings, carveouts, financings, and investments spanning dozens of industries and geographies.

Investor Mindset

We advise the way we would invest — with rigorous analysis, creative structuring, and a focus on alignment and outcomes over activity.

Senior Attention

Our most experienced professionals lead every engagement from origination through execution. We don't delegate to junior teams.

Creative Structuring

We thrive in complexity. When a transaction requires a bespoke structure, we have the experience and flexibility to design it.

Discretion

We are experienced working quietly to resolve situations comprehensively — and our clients trust us with their most sensitive matters.

Long-Term Orientation

We measure success by outcomes, not volume. Our client relationships are built to endure beyond any single transaction.
