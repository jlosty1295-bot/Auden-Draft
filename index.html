Why Auden

Aligned interests.
Unconflicted advice.

Auden Partners was founded on a straightforward belief: the best outcomes come when advisors and investors share the same incentives as their clients, whether advising, investing, or doing both. We take on a focused number of mandates, engaging senior professionals at every stage and bringing creative, unconventional thinking to challenging situations where conventional approaches have fallen short.

Selectivity & Senior Attention

Auden Partners takes on engagements selectively, and only where we believe we can make a meaningful impact. Every client receives the full attention of our senior team from inception through execution.

Investor Mindset Meets Advisory Expertise

Auden Partners uniquely combines active principal investing with restructuring advisory. We evaluate every engagement through the lens of investors putting real capital at risk, bringing a depth of judgment that purely fee-driven advisors cannot replicate.

Clear Alignment of Incentives

We structure our economics to reflect results, linking a significant share of compensation to value realized. Our interests are aligned with clients' ultimate objectives, not just with closing a transaction.

Discreet, Boutique Service

Our size is a strategic asset. We operate discreetly, protecting client confidentiality and managing sensitive stakeholder dynamics with the care and discretion that high-stakes transactions demand.

Proven at the Highest Level

The Auden Partners team has led some of the most demanding capital structure transactions in recent history (~$550 billion in value), earning multiple industry awards. Clients receive the sophistication and judgment of a top-tier platform, without bureaucracy or diffusion of attention.

Built from Conviction

Each of us left distinguished careers at world-class institutions, not for lack of opportunity, but to build something better. Auden was founded on the conviction that a smaller, more intentional platform can deliver meaningfully better outcomes for clients who need genuine focus, creativity, and alignment.

Our Philosophy

Core principles
we stand for.

A set of principles guides how we work with clients, investors, and partners.

Our skin is always in the game.

We are selective about the engagements and investments we take on, entering only where we can contribute meaningful value. Where feasible, we align ourselves with clients by taking a meaningful portion of our compensation in deal currency, tying our success to their outcomes rather than to transaction completion.

We are a we firm, not an I firm.

We are partners with our clients, our colleagues, and our investors. We treat one another with respect, honesty, and transparency. Individual contributions matter, but it is how we work together to deliver the best outcomes for our clients that defines us.

Our reputation is our most important asset.

It is the hardest to gain and the easiest to lose. Ethics, honesty, and integrity are the core of our firm. Every member of our team must act with the highest standards at all times. Our word is our bond.

We put our clients and investors first.

We listen carefully, and when our clients succeed, our own success follows. Every action we take must reflect this. Each client and investor should be our best reference for our next relationship.

We provide only the best.

We aim to be the best in everything we do and hold one another to that standard: excellent client service, insightful and unbiased advice, and outstanding work product. We demand deep thought, creativity, and innovation from each of our people, and we are always honest about what we can and cannot do.

We make our mistakes right.

If we make a mistake, we own it. We work together to develop a solution and use the experience to improve. We do not assign blame; we fix the problem and move forward stronger.

We invest patiently and think big.

We focus on long-term outcomes, committing our capital and time to opportunities with the potential to compound meaningfully over time. Our ambition is limitless, and we recognize that realizing it requires patience, focus, and perspective.

Our people are the backbone of everything we do.

We win because of our people and seek these values in each of them. We invest fully in supporting our team, offer superior opportunities for growth and advancement, and share our success generously. We compensate based on firm performance and individual contribution, without regard to background, belief, or practice.

We treat our investors' capital as our own.

We deploy capital based on compelling risk-adjusted returns, partnering with companies and management teams that share our values. We build value by investing wisely for the long term, and we make monetization decisions based on value, not outside pressure.

The Name

Auden /ˈɔː·dən/ prop. n.

Old English  "wise old friend"
Latin root  audire, to hear and to listen

The name Auden evokes a wise and trusted voice in demanding moments. At its root, "aud," meaning to hear and to listen, reflects our belief that the best advice begins with genuine understanding. We listen first, then counsel. Experienced advice, aligned capital, and long-term partnership.
