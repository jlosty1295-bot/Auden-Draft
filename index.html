Our Approach

Aligned interests.
Unconflicted advice.

Auden Partners was founded on a straightforward belief: the best outcomes come when advisors and investors share the same incentives as their clients, whether advising, investing, or doing both. We take on a focused number of mandates, engaging senior professionals at every stage and bringing creative, unconventional thinking to complex situations where conventional approaches have fallen short.

Selectivity & Senior Attention

Auden Partners takes on engagements selectively, and only where we believe we can make a meaningful impact. Every client receives the full attention of our senior team from inception through execution.

Investor Mindset Meets Advisory Expertise

Auden Partners uniquely combines active principal investing with restructuring advisory. We evaluate every situation through the lens of investors putting real capital at risk, bringing a depth of judgment that purely fee-driven advisors cannot replicate.

Clear Alignment of Incentives

We structure our economics to reflect results, linking a significant share of compensation to value realized. Our interests are aligned with clients' ultimate objectives, not just with closing a transaction.

Discreet, Boutique Service

Our size is a strategic asset. We operate discreetly, protecting client confidentiality and managing sensitive stakeholder dynamics with the care and discretion that high-stakes transactions demand.

Proven at the Highest Level

The Auden Partners team has led some of the most complex capital structure transactions in history (~$550 billion in value), earning multiple industry awards. Clients receive the sophistication and judgment associated with top-tier advisory platforms, without bureaucracy or diffusion of attention.
